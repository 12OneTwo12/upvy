**Upvy Account Deletion Guide**

---

## App Information

- **App Name**: Upvy
- **Developer**: Upvy Team

---

## How to Delete Your Account

### Method 1: Delete Directly in the App (Recommended)

1. Open the Upvy app
2. Tap the **Profile** icon in the bottom tab
3. Tap **Settings** (gear icon) in the top right corner
4. Scroll to the bottom of the screen
5. Tap **Delete Account** below the Logout button
6. Tap **Delete** in the confirmation popup to delete your account

### Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion via email:

- **Email**: app.grow.snap@gmail.com
- **Subject**: [Account Deletion Request] {Your registered email address}
- **Body**: Please include the email address you used to sign up and the reason for your deletion request

For email requests, we may ask for additional information for identity verification. Your request will be processed within 7 days after verification.

---

## Data That Will Be Deleted

The following data will be **immediately and permanently deleted** when you delete your account:

| Data Type | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Account Information | Email, social login connection info |
| Profile Information | Nickname, profile picture, bio |
| Activity Records | Likes, comments, saved content, followers/following |
| Viewing History | Content viewing history, recommendation algorithm data |
| Uploaded Content | All videos and quizzes you uploaded |
| Notification Settings | Push notification tokens and settings |

---

## Data That Will Be Retained

Some data may be retained for a certain period after deletion as required by law:

| Data Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|-----------|------------------|-------------|
| Service Usage Logs | 3 months | Communications Privacy Act |
| Report/Sanction Records | 1 year | Fraud Prevention |

Data will be automatically destroyed after the retention period expires.

---

## Important Notes

- Account deletion is **irreversible**
- You will not be able to log in with the deleted account
- If you sign up again with the same social account, a new account will be created
- Deletion may take up to 24 hours to process
