# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 10, 2025**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Upvy collects the following information through social login (OAuth 2.0):

- **Email address** (required)
- **Profile information** (nickname, profile picture - optional)
- **Social login provider** (Google, Naver, Kakao)
- **Service usage records** (content views, likes, comments, saves)
- **Device information** (OS version, app version, device identifier)

---

## 2. Purpose of Use

The collected information is used only for the following purposes:

- User registration and login authentication
- Service provision and personalized content recommendations
- Service improvement and new feature development
- Customer inquiries and support
- Prevention of fraudulent use and service security

---

## 3. Retention Period

| Information Item | Retention Period |
|-----------------|------------------|
| Account information | Until account deletion |
| Service usage records | 3 years |
| Log data | 1 year |

All personal information is immediately deleted upon account deletion, except as required by law.

---

## 4. Third-Party Disclosure

Upvy does not provide personal information to third parties without user consent. However, the following cases are exceptions:

- When required by law
- When requested through legal procedures for investigation purposes
- When necessary for service provision (AWS, Kafka, and other infrastructure providers)

---

## 5. OAuth Social Login

Upvy does not store passwords and uses OAuth 2.0 protocol from Google, Naver, and Kakao.

- **Google**: Email address, profile information
- **Naver**: Email address, nickname, profile picture
- **Kakao**: Email address, nickname, profile picture

The privacy policies of each social login provider follow their respective service policies.

---

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Upvy uses the following technologies:

- **JWT tokens**: Maintain login status (Access Token 1 hour, Refresh Token 14 days)
- **Local storage**: App settings and cache data
- **Behavioral tracking**: Analyze viewing patterns for content recommendations (anonymized)

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Upvy does not collect personal information from children under 14 years of age. If parents or guardians believe their child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

---

## 8. User Rights

Users can exercise the following rights:

- **View**: Check your personal information
- **Edit**: Change profile information
- **Delete**: Delete account (Settings > Delete Account)
- **Stop processing**: Request temporary account suspension

---

## 9. Security

Upvy takes the following measures to safely protect personal information:

- TLS 1.3 encrypted communication
- Password bcrypt hashing (passwords not stored due to OAuth usage)
- AWS security infrastructure utilization
- Regular security checks and updates

---

## 10. Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be revised according to laws or service changes. Important changes will be announced at least 30 days in advance through in-service notifications.

---
