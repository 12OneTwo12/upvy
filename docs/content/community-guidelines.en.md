## Content Guidelines

### âœ… Encouraged Content

We welcome content that:
- Provides educational value (tutorials, tips, insights)
- Shares personal growth experiences and lessons learned
- Offers constructive feedback and discussions
- Celebrates achievements and milestones
- Inspires and motivates others
- Respects copyright and intellectual property

### âŒ Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited:

**Violence & Harm:**
- Content that promotes or glorifies violence
- Self-harm or suicide-related content
- Dangerous challenges or stunts that could cause injury

**Hate Speech & Discrimination:**
- Content that attacks individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality
- Slurs, derogatory language, or discriminatory remarks

**Sexual Content:**
- Sexually explicit content or nudity
- Content that sexualizes minors
- Sexual harassment or unwanted advances

**Illegal Activities:**
- Content promoting illegal drugs or controlled substances
- Instructions for illegal activities
- Sale of regulated goods (weapons, alcohol, tobacco)

**Misinformation:**
- Deliberately false or misleading information
- Medical misinformation that could cause harm
- Content manipulated to deceive (deepfakes without disclosure)

**Harassment & Bullying:**
- Targeted harassment of individuals
- Cyberbullying or intimidation
- Doxxing (sharing private information without consent)

**Spam & Scams:**
- Repetitive, irrelevant content
- Pyramid schemes or get-rich-quick scams
- Phishing attempts or malicious links

**Copyright Infringement:**
- Content that violates others' intellectual property rights
- Unauthorized use of copyrighted material
- Impersonation of others

## Community Behavior

### Be Respectful

- Engage in constructive discussions
- Disagree politely and professionally
- Value different opinions and perspectives
- Give credit to original creators

### Be Authentic

- Share genuine experiences and knowledge
- Disclose sponsored content or partnerships
- Don't mislead others with fake accounts or credentials

### Be Responsible

- Think before you post
- Consider the impact of your content on others
- Report violations when you see them
- Protect your privacy and others'

## Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines:

1. **In-App Reporting**
   - Tap the "â‹¯" menu on any content
   - Select "Report"
   - Choose the violation type
   - Provide additional details (optional)

2. **Moderation Review**
   - Reports are reviewed within 24-48 hours
   - Automated systems may take immediate action for severe violations
   - You'll receive updates on your report

3. **Appeals**
   - If your content was removed, you can appeal the decision
   - Go to Settings > Help & Support > Appeal Content Removal
   - Explain why you believe the content doesn't violate guidelines

### Enforcement Actions

Depending on the severity and frequency of violations, we may take the following actions:

**First Violation (Minor):**
- Warning notification
- Content removal
- Educational resources

**Repeat Violations (Moderate):**
- Temporary account suspension (3-7 days)
- Content creation restrictions
- Comment restrictions

**Severe Violations:**
- Permanent account ban
- Device ban for extreme cases
- Report to law enforcement if illegal activity is involved

### User Safety Tools

Protect yourself with these features:

- **Block Users**: Prevent specific users from interacting with you
- **Content Filters**: Customize what content you see
- **Comment Controls**: Control who can comment on your content
- **Privacy Settings**: Manage who can see your profile and content

Upvy is committed to protecting minors:
- Users must be 14 years or older
- No content featuring minors in dangerous situations
- No content that sexualizes or exploits minors
- Parents/guardians can report concerns to support
