## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Account & Login

**Q: I can't log in.**
A: Upvy uses Google social login. Please check your Google account connection status in account settings.

**Q: I want to delete my account.**
A: Go to App Settings > Account Management > Delete Account. All your data will be permanently deleted.

### Content & Features

**Q: I can't upload videos.**
A: Please check the following:
- Photo library access permission is granted
- Video file size is under 100MB
- Stable network connection

**Q: Where can I see my liked/saved content?**
A: Go to Profile > Saved tab to view them.

### Other

**Q: The app keeps crashing.**
A: Update to the latest version or reinstall the app. If the problem persists, contact us via email below.
